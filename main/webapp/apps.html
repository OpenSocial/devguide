Hello World App

Introduction to the Gadget XML

The root of any OpenSocial App is the Gadget XML file.

HelloWorld.xml

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8" ?>
<Module>
  <ModulePrefs title="Hello World!">
    <Require feature="opensocial-0.8" />
  </ModulePrefs>
  <Content type="html">
    <![CDATA[
      Hello, OpenSocial Gadget world!
    ]]>
  </Content>
</Module>
